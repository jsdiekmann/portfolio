About Me

Hi! My name is Jared and I am an experienced service profesional, looking to transition in to the software engineering world! I started my career as a high school football coach and teacher. I then began chasing my dream of being a college football coach.

My girlfriend at the time (now wife) and I packed up and moved to Chicago, where I was the tight ends coach at Division III Elmhurst College. From there, the chase led us to beautiful Cape Girardeau, Missouri.

Slowly, I began to realize that my 22 year old dream of becoming a big time college football coach, was no longer the dream of my 32 year old self. Fast forward 4 years and the joy I get from solving puzzles has led me to the world of software engineering!
